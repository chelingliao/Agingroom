Aging Room Cigar & Spirits

為熱情而生

就像雪茄隨著時間而調和出豐富與深層的風味，「友誼」也是需要藉著時間的推移而變得可靠與堅定。
醇化與調和就是Aging Room的主要功能，在這裡，組成雪茄需使用的不同類型菸草，
透過濕度與溫度的加成效果，在歲月靜好的氛圍中，自然就完美平衡地結合了。
而一支雪茄需要茄心、茄套、外包葉的組成才能完整，就如同來自四面八方的人們聚集在
Aging Room一樣，因為他們，這空間才稱完整。

在這空間的每個成員，互動的願望都標誌著友誼 - 這是我們對彼此的經驗和價值觀所產生的興趣。
人們來這尋找「歸屬感」和「聯繫」的感覺。“就像雪茄在Aging Room 中隨著時間
更臻完美並豐富其風味底蘊。

Just like cigars, it improves with age, "Friendship" is also enriched over time.
This is the major mission of the "Aging room" where the humidity levels of
the different types of tobaccos used in a cigarare combined.
In other words, the humidity levels in the tobaccos of the wrapper,
filler and binder come together just as folks gathering in one room.
Friendships are built by each member's desire to engage with the each other
– it's about mutual interest in one another experiences and thoughts,
as well as a sense of 'belongingness' and connection.", just like
cigars which will improve and enrich over time in The Aging room.
